Our History!

Tekton was founded in 1952 in West Lafayette, Indiana by Taylor Tekton. Taylor got his start in the consturction industry in 1948 when he joined Steinberger Construction as an apprentice. Taylor possessed great leadership, adaptability, and an unmatched determination for project accuracy and satisfaction. Values we still share at Tekton to this day! Tayler was soon promoted to project liasion at Steinberger, and one year later chose to leave Steiberger to start his own construction company, and Tekton was born!
